DSAC Telemetry Filter

This program is used to filter out data packets
from the Deep Space Atomic Clock that fit into a user-selected range. It
then prints out these packets to a spreadsheet. I wrote this as I learned to
code during my internship at the Jet Propulsion Laboratory as part of its
Summer High School Internship Program under NASA's Minority
University Research and Education Program.

Lumboard

The main purpose of Lumboard is to help users text more
quickly and easily. It provides the comfort of spelling out words that
are usually shortened, all with the tap of one key. The keyboard's design
was inspired by Matias Duarte's Material Design. This application was
built at the University of Waterloo at Hack The North.
